About

Hi & Welcome:

I'm Arthur. I've been intrigued by programming ever since the 80s in my teenage when I laid my first lines of BASIC on a Commodore 64. Wait..., not another sentimental history lesson. I'm not that old. I've actually just began programming at the end of university on a modern PC in 2021 chose embedded systems over other fields. Since then, I've been writing code for different microcontrollers on different projects as an embedded systems engineer for Run Automations in Uganda on training and on different side gigs.

On this personal blog, I write about embedded systems based on my own projects. Why writing? Because writing makes me aware of my thoughts and there is no better tool for organizing a long line of thought. It's also healthy and useful to share thoughts with others. Why embedded systems? I get more satisfaction from controlling a piece of hardware than building an app and for this kind of craft in Uganda, there is less support, guidance or mentorship to any rookie and this is in that innitiative too. I'll help others learn as I do learn.
